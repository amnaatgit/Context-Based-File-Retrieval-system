EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
Finance Department | Last Updated: February 2025

ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
- Business travel (flights, trains, accommodation)
- Client entertainment (with prior manager approval)
- Professional development and training
- Office supplies purchased on behalf of the company
- Conference registration fees

APPROVAL THRESHOLDS
- Up to PKR 5,000: Manager approval only
- PKR 5,001 – 25,000: Manager + Finance approval
- Above PKR 25,000: Director sign-off required

SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
1. Complete the expense claim form (available on intranet)
2. Attach all original receipts (scanned or photographed)
3. Include business justification for each item
4. Submit within 30 days of expense date

TRAVEL POLICY
- Economy class for domestic and regional flights
- Business class permitted for international flights over 6 hours
- Hotel budget: PKR 15,000/night domestic, USD 150/night international
- Mileage reimbursement: PKR 18/km for personal vehicle use

NON-REIMBURSABLE ITEMS
- Personal entertainment or gifts
- Alcohol purchases (unless client entertainment with approval)
- Traffic fines and penalties
- Expenses without valid receipts

PAYMENT TIMELINE
Approved claims processed within 10 working days.
Paid directly to employee's registered bank account.
Queries: finance@company.com
