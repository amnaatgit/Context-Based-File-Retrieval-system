# Annual Financial Statement 2024

## Executive Summary
This annual financial statement summarizes the company's financial performance for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2024. The finance department prepared this report for the board, auditors, and department heads. It covers revenue, expenses, payroll cost, profit margin, and variance analysis against the approved annual budget.

## Revenue Overview
Total revenue for 2024 reached PKR 480,000,000, a 22% increase over 2023. Recurring subscription revenue accounted for 61% of the total, while professional services and one-time licensing made up the remainder. Q4 was the strongest quarter, driven by enterprise contract renewals.

## Expense Allocation
- Payroll and benefits: PKR 210,000,000 (44% of expenses)
- Cloud infrastructure and IT: PKR 58,000,000
- Marketing and campaigns: PKR 42,000,000
- Facilities and operations: PKR 31,000,000
- Travel and training: PKR 12,000,000

## Profit and Variance Analysis
Operating profit was PKR 96,000,000 with a net margin of 20%. Actual spending came in 4% under the approved budget, mainly due to delayed hiring in Q2 and lower-than-forecast travel costs. Cloud spending exceeded forecast by 9% because of the server migration project.

## Forecast for 2025
The finance team projects 18% revenue growth for 2025, contingent on the Connect Forward marketing campaign and two planned product launches. A detailed quarterly budget forecast is maintained in the Q1 budget report.
