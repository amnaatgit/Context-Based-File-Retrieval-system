# Q1 Budget Report

## Overview
The finance department finalized the Q1 2025 budget report. This report covers revenue, expense allocation, payroll cost, and variance analysis against the approved annual budget. It is the primary quarterly financial document for department heads and the finance committee.

## Revenue
Q1 revenue reached PKR 118,000,000, tracking slightly ahead of the annual forecast. Subscription renewals were strong, and one large enterprise deal closed earlier than expected.

## Expense Allocation
- Payroll and benefits: PKR 52,000,000
- Cloud infrastructure and IT: PKR 15,000,000
- Marketing and campaigns: PKR 9,500,000
- Facilities and operations: PKR 7,000,000
- Travel and training: PKR 2,500,000

## Variance Analysis
Overall spending was 3% under budget for the quarter. Marketing spend was slightly below plan because the Connect Forward campaign starts in Q2. Cloud costs rose due to the ongoing server migration project.

## Payroll Cost
Payroll remains the largest single cost at 44% of expenses. Two planned hires in the engineering team were delayed to Q2, reducing payroll variance for this quarter.

## Forecast
The finance team maintains the full-year forecast of 18% revenue growth. A detailed annual view is available in the annual financial statement.
