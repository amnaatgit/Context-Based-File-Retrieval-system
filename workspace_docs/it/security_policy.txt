IT SECURITY POLICY
IT Department | Version 3.1 | March 2025

PASSWORD POLICY
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol
- Change every 90 days
- Do not reuse last 10 passwords
- Multi-factor authentication required for all cloud systems

ACCEPTABLE USE
Company devices are for business use. Limited personal use is permitted.
Do not download or install software without IT approval.
Do not connect company devices to public WiFi without VPN.
Screen lock must activate after 5 minutes of inactivity.

DATA CLASSIFICATION
CONFIDENTIAL: Financial records, HR data, client contracts
INTERNAL: Project documents, meeting notes, internal emails
PUBLIC: Marketing materials, published reports, press releases

Never share CONFIDENTIAL data externally without written authorisation.

INCIDENT REPORTING
Report security incidents IMMEDIATELY to: security@company.com
Include: date/time, systems affected, description of incident
Do not attempt to investigate or resolve incidents independently

REMOTE ACCESS
Use company VPN for all remote connections
Approved tools: Slack, Google Workspace, internal systems only
Personal cloud storage (Dropbox, personal Google Drive) not permitted for company files

DEVICE MANAGEMENT
Lost or stolen devices must be reported within 2 hours
IT will remotely wipe all data from reported lost devices
Do not attempt to repair company hardware independently

COMPLIANCE
This policy aligns with ISO 27001 guidelines.
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
Annual security training is mandatory for all staff.
