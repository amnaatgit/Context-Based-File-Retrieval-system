BRAND GUIDELINES — INTERNAL REFERENCE
Version 2.3 | Updated March 2025

PRIMARY COLOURS
- Indigo 600: #4F46E5 (primary brand)
- Emerald 500: #10B981 (success states)
- Slate 900: #0F172A (body text)

TYPOGRAPHY
- Headings: Inter Bold (700 weight)
- Body: Inter Regular (400 weight)
- Monospace: JetBrains Mono

TONE OF VOICE
Professional yet approachable. Avoid jargon. Lead with value, not features.
Always use active voice. Keep sentences under 20 words where possible.

LOGO USAGE
Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo.
Minimum size: 32px height in digital contexts.
Clear space: equal to the height of the logo mark on all sides.

DO NOT USE
- Clipart or stock photo people
- Comic Sans or decorative fonts
- More than 3 colours in a single graphic
- All-caps body text

SOCIAL MEDIA STANDARDS
LinkedIn: Formal, data-driven, case study focused
Twitter/X: Conversational, quick tips, company news
Instagram: Behind the scenes, team culture, visual product shots
