# Social Media Strategy 2025

## Owner
Mariam Noor, Marketing Lead, Growth team

## Goal
Grow the company's social presence to support the Connect Forward campaign and generate qualified leads. This strategy complements the Q2 campaign brief and shares its budget.

## Platform Focus
- LinkedIn: primary B2B channel, thought leadership and product news
- Instagram: culture, hiring, and brand storytelling
- YouTube: product demos, webinars, and customer stories
- X (Twitter): real-time updates and community engagement

## Content Pillars
1. Product education and feature launches
2. Customer success stories and case studies
3. Company culture and behind the scenes
4. Industry insights and trends

## Posting Cadence
- LinkedIn: 6 posts per week
- Instagram: 4 posts per week plus daily stories
- YouTube: 2 videos per month
- X: 1 to 2 posts per day

## Key Metrics
- Follower growth: 25% year over year
- Engagement rate target: 4.5%
- Leads from social: 300 per quarter
- Share of voice versus top three competitors: 30%

## Approval Workflow
Draft posts are reviewed by the Growth team lead. Product announcements also require sign-off from the relevant product owner before publishing.
