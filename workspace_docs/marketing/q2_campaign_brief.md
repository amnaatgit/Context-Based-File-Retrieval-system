# Q2 2025 Marketing Campaign Brief

## Campaign: "Connect Forward"
**Owner:** Mariam Noor, Marketing Lead
**Budget:** PKR 4,200,000
**Timeline:** April 1 – June 30, 2025

## Objectives
- Increase brand awareness by 35% across target demographics
- Generate 1,200 qualified leads through digital channels
- Achieve 18% email open rate (up from 14% in Q1)
- Launch two new product feature announcements

## Target Segments
1. Enterprise clients (500+ employees) in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad
2. Mid-market tech companies looking for SaaS solutions
3. Re-engagement of dormant customers from 2023–2024

## Channel Strategy
- LinkedIn sponsored content (40% of budget)
- Email nurture sequences (20% of budget)
- Google Ads — branded and competitor keywords (25% of budget)
- Event sponsorship — TechConnect Karachi (15% of budget)

## Key Deliverables
- 6 LinkedIn posts per week
- 2 email newsletters per month
- Landing page redesign by April 15
- Product launch webinar — May 20

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Q1 Actual | Q2 Target |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Leads | 880 | 1,200 |
| Email CTR | 3.2% | 4.5% |
| Ad ROAS | 2.8x | 3.5x |
