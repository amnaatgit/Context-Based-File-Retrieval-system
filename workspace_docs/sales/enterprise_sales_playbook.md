# Enterprise Sales Playbook 2025

## Purpose
This playbook guides the enterprise sales team through the full deal cycle, from prospecting to closed-won. It standardizes discovery, proposal, and negotiation so every account executive follows a repeatable, context-aware process.

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Companies with 500+ employees
- Annual technology budget above PKR 50,000,000
- Existing SaaS adoption and a dedicated procurement team
- Priority regions: Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Dubai

## Sales Stages
1. Prospecting: identify accounts, research stakeholders, log activity in CRM
2. Discovery: run a needs-analysis call, document pain points and success metrics
3. Proposal: send a tailored proposal with pricing tiers and an ROI summary
4. Negotiation: align on contract terms, security review, and legal redlines
5. Close: obtain signature, hand off to onboarding, schedule kickoff

## Proposal Guidelines
Every proposal must reference the client review budget notes, include a clear scope of work, and map deliverables to the client's stated objectives. Standard payment terms are net-30. Discounts above 12% require sales director approval.

## Objection Handling
- "Too expensive": reframe around annual value and total cost of ownership
- "We already have a vendor": highlight migration support and integration coverage
- "Not the right time": propose a phased rollout starting next quarter

## Key Metrics
- Target quota per AE: PKR 60,000,000 annually
- Average deal size: PKR 5,000,000
- Target win rate: 28%
- Sales cycle length: 45 to 90 days
