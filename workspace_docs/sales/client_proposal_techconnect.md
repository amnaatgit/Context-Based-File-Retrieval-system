# Client Proposal: TechConnect Solutions

## Prepared For
TechConnect Solutions (Karachi) — 620 employees
Primary contact: Faisal Mahmood, VP of Operations

## Prepared By
Bilal Ahmed, Enterprise Sales Director

## Overview
This proposal outlines a partnership to deploy our platform across TechConnect's operations, sales, and support teams. It follows the discovery calls held in March 2025 and directly addresses the objectives shared by their leadership.

## Client Objectives
- Reduce document retrieval time for support agents
- Centralize scattered files across five departments
- Improve onboarding speed for new hires
- Meet internal security and compliance requirements

## Proposed Scope
1. Platform deployment for 620 users
2. Migration of existing document archive (approx. 45,000 files)
3. Integration with existing single sign-on
4. Custom department-based access rules
5. Admin training and 90 days of premium support

## Pricing
- Annual platform license: PKR 9,600,000
- One-time migration and setup: PKR 1,800,000
- Premium support (year one included, then PKR 1,200,000/year)
- Volume discount applied: 10%

## ROI Summary
Based on current retrieval times, we project a 60% reduction in time spent locating documents, saving an estimated 4,800 staff hours annually. Payback period is approximately 11 months.

## Next Steps
Signature target: April 30, 2025. Onboarding kickoff within two weeks of signing.
