# Data Privacy Policy

## Scope
This policy governs how the company collects, stores, processes, and shares personal data. It applies to all employees, contractors, and systems that handle customer or employee information. It is maintained by the Legal and Compliance team.

## Principles
1. Collect only the data needed for a clear business purpose
2. Store data securely and limit access by role and department
3. Retain data only as long as necessary
4. Give individuals the right to access and correct their data
5. Report any data breach within the required timeframe

## Data Categories
- Customer data: contact details, contracts, and usage records
- Employee data: payroll, performance, and personal records
- Operational data: logs, analytics, and system metadata

## Access Controls
Access to personal data follows the principle of least privilege. Department-based rules restrict who can view sensitive records. All access is logged for audit purposes.

## Retention Schedule
- Customer contracts: 7 years after contract end
- Employee records: 7 years after employment ends
- System logs: 12 months
- Marketing consent records: until consent is withdrawn

## Breach Response
Suspected breaches must be reported to the Compliance Officer immediately. The incident response process follows the IT incident playbook, and affected individuals are notified as required by law.

## Review
This policy is reviewed annually and after any major regulatory change.

Owner: Legal and Compliance team
