EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — SUMMARY
Effective Date: January 2025 | HR Department

WORKING HOURS
Standard hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Flexible working arrangements require manager approval in writing.
Remote work up to 2 days per week is permitted after 3 months of employment.

LEAVE POLICY (Summary — see hr/leave_policy.txt for full details)
Annual Leave: 18 days per calendar year
Sick Leave: 10 days per year (medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days)
Casual Leave: 5 days per year (max 2 consecutive days)
Public Holidays: As per government gazette

CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to maintain professional behaviour at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are grounds for immediate termination.
Confidential company information must not be shared externally without approval.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Formal reviews are conducted bi-annually (June and December).
Mid-cycle check-ins are encouraged with direct managers.
Promotion criteria are based on performance ratings, tenure, and role availability.

EXPENSE CLAIMS
All business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence.
Receipts are required for any claim above PKR 500.
Submit via the Finance portal or email finance@company.com.

IT & DATA SECURITY
Do not install unauthorised software on company devices.
Use VPN when accessing company systems remotely.
Report any security incidents immediately to IT (helpdesk@company.com).

GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
Step 1: Raise informally with direct manager.
Step 2: If unresolved, submit written complaint to HR within 7 days.
Step 3: HR investigates and provides written response within 14 working days.
Step 4: Escalate to senior management panel if unresolved.
