# New Employee Onboarding Checklist

## Week 1 — Administration
- [ ] Submit CNIC copy to HR
- [ ] Sign employment contract and NDA
- [ ] Set up company email and Slack
- [ ] Complete payroll bank details form
- [ ] Receive equipment (laptop, accessories)
- [ ] Meet with direct manager (Day 1)
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training

## Week 1 — Orientation
- [ ] Company overview session with Ali Raza, HR
- [ ] Department introduction meetings
- [ ] Review company handbook (see general/company_handbook.txt)
- [ ] Tour of office facilities
- [ ] IT security briefing

## Week 2 — Role Onboarding
- [ ] Shadow a senior team member for 3 days
- [ ] Complete role-specific tool training
- [ ] First 1:1 with manager (recurring weekly)
- [ ] Set 30-60-90 day goals with manager
- [ ] Join relevant Slack channels and mailing lists

## Week 3–4 — Integration
- [ ] Attend first team stand-up
- [ ] Complete first independent task
- [ ] Mid-probation check-in with HR
- [ ] Access systems review — confirm all permissions correct
