HR Leave Policy

Purpose
This policy explains how employees request and take leave. It applies to all full-time and part-time staff and is maintained by the People Success team.

Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave requests must be submitted at least five working days in advance. The HR manager approves leave based on staffing needs and policy compliance.

Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For absences longer than two consecutive days, a medical certificate is required.

Parental Leave
The company offers 90 days of maternity leave and 15 days of paternity leave, in line with local labor law. Requests should be made at least four weeks before the expected start date.

Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be granted at the manager's discretion for exceptional circumstances, subject to HR approval.

Public Holidays
The company observes all national public holidays. A holiday calendar is published at the start of each year.

Carry Over
Employees may carry over a maximum of five unused annual leave days into the next year. Any remaining balance expires at year end.
